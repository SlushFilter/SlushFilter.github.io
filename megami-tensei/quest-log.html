### Megami Tensei I & II ###
Welcome in! We're playing a translated version
of Megami Tensei for the Super Famicom!

If you would like to play, just enter in
your button commands in chat and the inputs
will be sent to the game!

Command Summary
Chat Command Button
U D L R D-Pad Buttons
A B X Y Face Buttons
STA SEL Start & Select
L1 R1 Shoulder Buttons

Good Luck!
