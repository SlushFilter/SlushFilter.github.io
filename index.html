Oops! Ken Hyabusa forgot to pack the Dragon Sword for Ryu on his legendary journey to the Americas!
Are you a swaggy enough ninja to defeat The Jaquio and his goons with just the Windmill Shuriken?

This is a challenge mod for Ninja Gaiden (NES) that removes all of Ryu's weapons except for the Swag Star.
However, we've added a couple of extra features though to keep gameplay fun.
  • The Dragon Sword has been disabled.
  • All subweapons have been removed, except for the windmill shuriken.
  • B now throws a windmill shuriken.
  • Up + B now throws a "boost" windmill shuriken, causing a damage boost when it is caught.
  • Multiple swag stars now allowed onscreen via I, II, III and IV powerups.
[ Release Date : 7-17-21 ]
